📏 Formatting Guides for Sworn Translations

General Structure

  • Title: "Sworn Translation" + language pair
  • Certification statement at end
  • Translator's signature and seal
  • Date and place of certification
  • Page numbering (X of Y)
  • Attachment of original document

Certification Wording (EN→ES)

  • State translator's full name and appointment number
  • Include "traductor jurado" status reference
  • Declare faithful and complete translation
  • Include "a mi leal saber y entender" clause
  • Sign before notary when required
  • Use official stamp/seal

Formatting Rules

  • Preserve original document layout
  • Use same paragraph and page breaks
  • Maintain signature and seal images
  • Include marginal annotations where relevant
  • Use Times New Roman or similar serif
  • Numbered exhibits and appendices
