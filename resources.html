Official Bodies (6)

Spain

Ministry of Foreign Affairs, EU and Cooperation

Spain's official body responsible for the appointment and regulation of sworn translators and interpreters (Traductores/as e Intérpretes Jurados/as). Maintains the official register and exam schedules.

Visit Website →
EU

European Commission — Directorate-General for Translation (DGT)

The largest translation service in the world, employing over 2,500 linguists. Publishes style guides, terminology resources, and quality standards for EU legal translation.

Visit Website →
UK

UK Home Office — Approved Translator Register

Official register of translators and interpreters approved to provide services for UK visa and immigration applications. Mandatory registration for all sworn/legal translation work related to UK immigration.

Visit Website →
EU

Court of Justice of the European Union (CJEU) — Translation Directorate

The CJEU's in-house translation service handles legal translation for all EU judicial proceedings. Publishes a multilingual legal terminology database and style guide.

Visit Website →
Spain

Spanish Ministry of Justice

Government body overseeing the Spanish judicial system, including official court interpreters and translation standards for legal proceedings in Spain.

Visit Website →
International

World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) — Translation Division

WIPO's translation division handles patent and IP translations across 10+ languages. Their terminology database (WIPO Pearl) is an essential resource for patent translators.

Visit Website →

Legal Databases (5)

EU

EUR-Lex

Official online portal to EU law, providing access to the Official Journal of the EU, treaties, legislation, case law, and legislative proposals in all 24 official EU languages. Indispensable for EU legal translation.

Visit EUR-Lex →
Spain

BOE — Boletín Oficial del Estado

Spain's official state gazette publishing all legislation, royal decrees, ministerial orders, and official announcements. Essential for sworn translators requiring accurate legal references and terminology.

Visit BOE →
Spain

CENDOJ — Centro de Documentación Judicial

The Spanish Judicial Documentation Centre provides free access to the complete jurisprudence of all Spanish courts, including the Supreme Court and Constitutional Court. Critical for legal translation research.

Visit CENDOJ →
UK

BAILII — British and Irish Legal Information Institute

Free online database of UK and Irish legal materials including case law, legislation, and tribunal decisions. Key resource for EN→ES legal translation of common law terminology.

Visit BAILII →
International

World Legal Information Institute (WorldLII)

Global portal providing access to legal materials from over 120 countries. Useful for comparative legal terminology research when translating between multiple legal systems.

Visit WorldLII →

Professional Associations (5)

Spain

APTIC — Asociación Profesional de Traductores e Intérpretes de Cataluña

Professional association representing translators and interpreters in Catalonia. Provides networking, training, and certification resources. Strong focus on sworn/legal translation standards.

Visit APTIC →
Spain

AGTTI — Agrupación de Traductores e Intérpretes Jurados

National association of sworn translators and interpreters in Spain. Advocates for professional standards, publishes industry guidelines, and organises continuing education for sworn translators.

Visit AGTTI →
EU

EUATC — European Union of Associations of Translation Companies

Europe-wide umbrella organisation for translation company associations. Sets quality standards (EN 15038, ISO 17100), publishes industry benchmarks, and promotes ethical business practices.

Visit EUATC →
International

FIT — International Federation of Translators

Global federation of translator associations representing over 80,000 translators in 55+ countries. Publishes the Translator's Charter and organises the World Congress of Translation Studies.

Visit FIT →
UK

ITI — Institute of Translation and Interpreting

UK's leading professional membership body for translators and interpreters. Offers the prestigious MITI qualification and publishes the Bulletin magazine with regular legal translation features.

Visit ITI →

Tools & Glossaries (6)

IATE — Interactive Terminology for Europe

The EU's inter-institutional terminology database containing over 8 million entries in all 24 official EU languages. The gold standard for legal and institutional translation terminology.

Visit IATE →
EU

EURAMIS — European Advanced Multilingual Information System

EU's integrated translation environment including translation memories, terminology management, and quality assurance tools. Accessible to EU institutions and approved external contractors.

Linguee

Web-based bilingual concordancer that searches millions of professionally translated sentences. Particularly useful for legal translation to verify terminology in real-world context.

EU Terminology Portal

Central access point for all EU terminology resources, including glossaries, term lists, and specialised vocabularies across all policy areas. Searchable by domain and language pair.

Collaboration between the Royal Spanish Academy and the EFE Agency providing authoritative guidance on legal Spanish usage, terminology, and style. Essential for polishing legal translations into Spanish.
