Upload a TMX file to extract terminology terms. The system will automatically detect terms, context, and exclude sensitive information.

Max 50MB · Supports TMX v1.4 · UTF-8 encoded

📋 How it works
  1. The system reads your TMX file and extracts all bilingual units
  2. Short segments (1-4 words) are treated as terms
  3. Longer segments are treated as context and attached to related short terms
  4. Sensitive data (company names, addresses, emails, etc.) is automatically excluded
  5. You can review and manage the exclusion list in the Exclusions tab
